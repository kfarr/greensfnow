üéØ Project Goals

  • Stormwater Management: Install bioswales and permeable surfaces to reduce runoff
  • Community Space: Create a gathering place for neighborhood events and activities
  • Urban Heat Reduction: Add trees and vegetation to cool the urban environment
  • Air Quality: Improve local air quality through increased vegetation
  • Safe Streets: Implement traffic calming measures and improved pedestrian safety
  • Biodiversity: Introduce native plants to support local wildlife
