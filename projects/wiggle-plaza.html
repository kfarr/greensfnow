üå± Project Overview

Wiggle Plaza extends Duboce Park eastward, creating an accessible space for all ages and abilities while managing stormwater runoff from both Duboce Park and Buena Vista Park. This project transforms a critical section of the Wiggle bike route into a model of deep greening and permeable surface infrastructure.

üéØ Why This Location is Ideal

  • Stormwater Management: Captures rainwater flowing down from Buena Vista Park and Duboce Park, reducing strain on the sewer system
  • Equity & Access: Serves hundreds of elderly, disabled, minority, LGBTQ+, and low-income neighbors in nearby Bridge Buildings
  • Active Transportation: Critical leg of the Wiggle bike route used by families and commuters daily
  • Tourism & Economy: Part of proposed SF Caf√© District with tree-lined streets and Victorian architecture
  • Education: Adjacent to St. Francis Day School and McKinnely Elementary - perfect for children learning to bike
