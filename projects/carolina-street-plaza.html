Carolina Street Plaza

This project transforms Carolina Street into a green pedestrian corridor connecting Jackson Park to the Potrero Hill greenway network, creating a safe, accessible community space with bioswales and permeable surfaces for stormwater management

üå± Project Overview

Carolina Street Plaza envisions transforming one or more blocks of Carolina Street into a pedestrian-friendly green corridor that complements the ongoing Jackson Park improvements. The project emphasizes permeable surfaces, bioswales, street trees, and traffic calming measures that would make Carolina Street a distinctive neighborhood asset rather than an unnecessary through street.

üå≥ Why This Location is Ideal

  • Low Traffic Need: Most traffic on Carolina Street is parking-related; through traffic is minimal and unnecessary
  • Greenway Connectivity: Creates a continuous green corridor from the waterfront to the hilltop Carolina Green Space
  • School Access: Live Oak School and Potrero Hill Montessori students can safely walk through a green pedestrian-friendly environment from the school to programming at Jackson Park
  • Stormwater Management: Bioswales and permeable pavers can capture and filter stormwater runoff
  • Excess Width: Carolina Street is unnecessarily wide for its actual traffic needs, providing ample space for greening while maintaining necessary access
  • Development Coordination: Future development of Anchor Brewing properties presents opportunity for coordinated planning
  • Existing Momentum: Jackson Park improvements already underway, including planned 6-foot extension into Carolina Street from Mariposa down 200 feet
