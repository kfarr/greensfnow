Priority Projects

Discover our ongoing green infrastructure initiatives across San Francisco. Each project demonstrates how we can transform our city with sustainable, community-centered solutions.

Cortland in Bloom

📍 Bernal Heights • Cortland Avenue

Beautifies Cortland Ave, Bernal Heights' business district, and helps keep neighbors safe by promoting daylighting of intersections through strategic placement of potted plants.
